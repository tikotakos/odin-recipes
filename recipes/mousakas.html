Mousakas

Description

  • Mousakas is a traditional Greek dish made with layers of eggplant, ground meat, and béchamel sauce. It is baked to perfection and is a hearty and flavorful meal.
  • It is often served with a side of salad or crusty bread.
  • Enjoy this classic comfort food that brings the taste of Greece to your table!

    Ingridients

    • 2 large eggplants, sliced
    • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 cups tomato sauce
    • 1 cup béchamel sauce
